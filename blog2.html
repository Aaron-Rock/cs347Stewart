It is interesting just how valuable a good mobile and website correlates to the sales of a product on that company's site. According to a Forbes article, studies show that if a consumer deems a website to not look well designed or function correctly the there is a 46% decrease they will buy the product. What was facinating to me was the correlation between the quality of the website's function and whether the consumer would proceed with purchases. It shows that functionality is just as important to the consumer than the aesthetic. On the first day we went over what a mobile display and desktop display would look like if the network is throttled down. My belief after reading this article is that you want to have some base line functions working even if the network is slower than anticipated.
